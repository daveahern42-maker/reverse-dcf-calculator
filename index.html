Example Analysis

Apple (AAPL)
Try: Reverse DCF
Market is pricing in 8-12% annual growth. Compare this to Apple's historical growth and consider if they can sustain it with Services and new products.
Microsoft (MSFT)
Try: ROIC
Consistently generates ROIC > 40%, showing exceptional capital efficiency. Azure and cloud services demonstrate a strong competitive moat.
Google (GOOGL)
Try: ROIIC
ROIIC > 25% demonstrates Google can reinvest capital at extraordinary rates. Search and YouTube continue to provide massive opportunities.
Berkshire Hathaway
Try: WACC
Low WACC (~7-8%) due to stable business mix and strong balance sheet. Compare to ROIC of 12%+ to see consistent value creation.
